Popular Degree Courses After 2nd PUC

B.Sc – Bachelor of Science

Duration: 3 years

Description: Covers Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer Science.

Career Options: Researcher, Lab Technician, MSc, IT Industry, Teaching

B.Com – Bachelor of Commerce

Duration: 3 years

Description: Business, finance, economics, taxation, and accountancy studies.

Career Options: Accountant, CA, CS, Bank Officer, Financial Analyst

BA – Bachelor of Arts

Duration: 3 years

Description: Humanities stream including Psychology, History, Journalism, Sociology, etc.

Career Options: UPSC, Journalism, Teaching, MA, Law, Content Writing

BBA – Bachelor of Business Administration

Duration: 3 years

Description: Management and business fundamentals including HR, Marketing, Finance.

Career Options: Manager, HR Executive, MBA, Entrepreneur, Sales Analyst

BCA – Bachelor of Computer Applications

Duration: 3 years

Description: Computer programming, software development, and IT applications.

Career Options: Software Developer, Web Developer, IT Support, MCA

MBBS – Bachelor of Medicine & Bachelor of Surgery

Duration: 5.5 years

Description: Medical degree required to become a doctor and practice medicine.

Career Options: Doctor, Surgeon, Specialist, PG in MD/MS, Healthcare Professional

B.Tech – Bachelor of Technology

Duration: 4 years

Description: Engineering degree in fields like Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, etc.

Career Options: Software Engineer, Civil Engineer, Government Exams, MBA, M.Tech

BSW – Bachelor of Social Work

Duration: 3 years

Description: Social welfare and development, working in communities and NGOs.

Career Options: Social Worker, NGO Officer, MSW, Counselor, Child Welfare Officer
